# ❗ Problem Statement

## 📌 Current Scenario

- Manual verification hota hai  
- Time-consuming hota hai  
- Human judgement pe depend karta hai  

---

## ⚠️ Major Problems

### 1. Slow Processing
Loan approval me kaafi time lagta hai.

### 2. Human Bias
Different officers different decision lete hain.

### 3. Risk Prediction Difficulty
Default risk accurately identify karna mushkil hota hai.

### 4. Lack of Transparency
User ko nahi pata hota loan reject kyu hua.

---

## 💥 Impact

- Financial loss  
- Bad customer experience  
- Inconsistent decisions  

---

- Explainability provide karta hai

## ✅ Expected Outcome

- Faster loan approval

- Better transparency
